Understanding Sentence Types

Sentences can be classified into four main types based on their purpose and structure:

1. Declarative Sentences

These are statements that provide information. They typically end with a period (.).

Example: "The cat is sleeping on the couch."

2. Interrogative Sentences

These are questions that seek information. They always end with a question mark (?).

Example: "What time does the movie start?"

3. Imperative Sentences

These are commands or instructions. They can end with a period (.) or an exclamation point (!).

Example: "Close the door behind you."

4. Exclamatory Sentences

These express strong emotion or excitement. They always end with an exclamation point (!).

Example: "What an amazing performance!"
